Yipeng is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Dawu Gu (Changjiang Scholar, Dean of the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Vice President of the Chinese Association for Cryptologic Research).

His research focuses on white-box cryptography, physically unclonable function (PUF)-based security schemes, and symmetric cipher analysis and design. He is also broadly interested in physical attacks and countermeasures, cryptographic implementation, trusted computing systems, and the intersection of cryptography and neural networks.
