# Hello World — Welcome to My Blog

This is the first post on my personal blog. I plan to use this space to share thoughts and notes on topics I care about.

## What to Expect

- **White-box cryptography**: design principles, attack surfaces, and practical implementations
- **PUF-based security**: protocols, standards, and real-world deployments
- **Symmetric cipher analysis**: MILP-based cryptanalysis, lightweight primitives
- **Research notes**: paper summaries, experiment logs, and conference takeaways
- **Academic life**: tips for PhD students, reading lists, and tool recommendations

## Why a Blog?

Writing clarifies thinking. A blog forces me to organize scattered notes into coherent narratives — something that directly benefits research writing. It is also a nice way to share what I am learning with the broader crypto and security community.

## Technical Setup

This blog is built as a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. Blog posts are written in Markdown and stored in the `posts/` directory. A lightweight JavaScript engine fetches and renders them on the fly — no static site generator, no build step, just plain HTML/CSS/JS.

If you spot any issues or have suggestions, feel free to open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/siponline/siponline.github.io).
